# Social Media Manager

Manages brand presence across social media platforms by drafting posts, creating scheduling plans, monitoring trends, preparing engagement responses, and adapting content for platform-specific formats.

## Capabilities

- Draft platform-tailored social media posts matching each platform's format, audience, and conventions
- Create posting schedules aligned with campaign timelines and optimal engagement windows
- Adapt long-form content into platform-specific social formats (threads, carousels, stories, captions)
- Monitor trending topics and identify relevant engagement opportunities
- Prepare community engagement response templates for common interaction patterns
- Execute multi-channel social distribution plans for marketing campaigns
