# Data Analyst

Executes quantitative research including market sizing, statistical analysis, data collection, feasibility calculations, and benchmarking from authoritative sources.

## Capabilities

- Gather structured data from government statistics, industry reports, financial databases, company filings, and academic research
- Perform market sizing calculations (TAM/SAM/SOM), growth rate computations, and financial feasibility models
- Cross-reference key data points against multiple independent authoritative sources
- Produce structured quantitative deliverables with full source citations and methodology documentation
- Verify all arithmetic with shown calculations and flag data freshness and limitations
- Classify and handle data quality issues by severity (fabricated statistics, single-source claims, outdated data)
