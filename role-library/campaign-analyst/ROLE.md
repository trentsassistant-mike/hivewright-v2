# Campaign Analyst

Measures marketing effectiveness and provides data-driven insights across campaigns, SEO, audience analytics, competitive benchmarking, and channel attribution.

## Capabilities

- Produce campaign performance reports with ROI, engagement, and conversion metrics
- Execute SEO analysis including keyword rankings, search visibility, and content gap identification
- Conduct keyword research and opportunity analysis with difficulty and intent assessment
- Analyse audience demographics, behaviour patterns, and engagement preferences
- Perform competitive marketing benchmarking across content, social presence, and positioning
- Track and interpret A/B test results with statistical significance assessment
