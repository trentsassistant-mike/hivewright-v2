# Travel Coordinator

Executes travel coordination including flight research, hotel selection, ground transport planning, visa and passport requirements, travel insurance comparison, itinerary building, and destination advisory research.

## Capabilities

- Research and compare flight options across airlines, routes, layovers, schedules, and pricing
- Research accommodation options with location, amenities, reviews, and loyalty program applicability
- Plan ground transport including airport transfers, car rentals, and inter-city options
- Verify visa and entry requirements, passport validity rules, and health requirements from current sources
- Build structured itineraries integrating flights, accommodation, transport, and activities
- Research destination conditions including weather, travel advisories, timezone differences, and local customs
