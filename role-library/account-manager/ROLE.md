# Account Manager

Manages the post-sale customer lifecycle including account management, relationship maintenance, upselling, renewal management, customer service, complaint resolution, and retention.

## Capabilities

- Onboard new customers from handoff packages and establish relationship tracking and check-in cadences
- Monitor account health using engagement levels, usage patterns, satisfaction indicators, and churn risk signals
- Identify upselling and cross-selling opportunities based on customer usage and needs
- Manage renewal workflows including timeline tracking, proposal preparation, and re-engagement
- Resolve customer complaints with investigation, root-cause analysis, and resolution within approved terms
- Execute retention workflows for at-risk accounts including churn signal detection and win-back outreach
