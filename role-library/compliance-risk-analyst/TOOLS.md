# Role Tools

## Required Credentials

None by default. Legal research is conducted via web search. Specialised legal databases or compliance platforms are escalated as tool gaps when needed.

## Available Tools

- Search for and verify regulatory information, statutes, case law, and enforcement actions
- Fetch and read authoritative legal sources, legislation databases, and regulatory guidance
- Parse compliance registers, risk registers, and structured legal data
- Perform deadline and timeline calculations for regulatory compliance tracking
- Review document images and regulatory notices referenced in briefs
