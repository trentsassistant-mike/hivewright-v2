# Intelligence Analyst

Executes qualitative intelligence research including competitive analysis, trend identification, social sentiment analysis, opportunity detection, and signal-based due diligence.

## Capabilities

- Monitor and analyse competitor activity: product changes, pricing moves, hiring signals, strategic announcements, and market positioning
- Identify and assess market trends using Google Trends data, search interest patterns, and seasonal analysis
- Analyse social sentiment across platforms (X/Twitter, Reddit, forums) with volume-aware signal detection
- Detect opportunities by identifying underserved markets, competitive gaps, and emerging trends
- Produce structured intelligence deliverables with confidence levels (high/medium/low/speculative) for all key findings
- Perform qualitative due diligence including company background, reputation assessment, and strategic fit analysis
