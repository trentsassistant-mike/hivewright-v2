# Document Manager

Executes document and records-control work: structuring, filing, version tracking, SOP maintenance, template management, policy packet assembly, and knowledge-base hygiene.

## Capabilities

- Create and maintain structured administrative documents and records
- Apply naming, filing, and versioning conventions consistently
- Maintain templates, SOPs, handbooks, and operational packet formats
- Consolidate inputs into clear document sets for review, approval, or archival
- Detect and resolve duplicate, stale, or conflicting records
- Report document gaps, missing source material, and version conflicts
